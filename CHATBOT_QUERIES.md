## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: "Fees for BCA" works better than just "fees"
2. **Use Natural Language**: Ask questions naturally - the bot is smart!
3. **Try Variations**: If your exact phrasing doesn't work, try rephrasing
4. **Single Topics**: Better to ask one thing at a time for clarity
5. **Location Names**: Use correct spelling for campus locations
6. **Course Names**: Use official course names (BCA, BSC, etc.)

## ⚡ Quick Commands

Single-word queries for quick results:

| Command | Response |
|---------|----------|
| `menu` | Shows entire canteen menu |
| `holidays` | Lists all holidays |
| `fees` | Shows fees for all courses |
| `seats` | Shows seat availability for all courses |
| `strength` | Shows enrollment strength for all courses |

---

## ❌ Things Chatbot Can't Help With

- **Personal Messages**: "Tell my friend..."
- **Outside Campus**: "What's the weather?" (unless campus-related)
- **Account Management**: Password resets (use profile/settings)
- **Real-time Support**: Live chat with admin
- **Inappropriate Requests**: Harmful or offensive queries
